  • Create a quantum circuit with 2 qubits and 2 classical bits.
  • Apply a Hadamard gate to qubit 0 to create superposition.
  • Perform a controlled-X (CNOT) operation with qubit 0 as the control and qubit 1 as the target. This creates entanglement between the two qubits.
  • Measure the qubits and store the results in the classical bits.
